Help & User Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to our boxing video analysis and training platform! Here's how to make the most out of our features:

  1. Registration: Create a new account by clicking on "Register" from the main page. Provide accurate information for personalized training.
  2. Login: After registration, log in using your credentials to access the main dashboard and training modules.
  3. Profile Management: Visit the profile page to view or update your personal information such as username, password, email, and phone number.
  4. Video Analysis: Choose between "Live Input" to use your webcam for real-time boxing analysis or "Video Input" to upload recorded boxing footage for detailed feedback.
  5. Training Modules: Explore different boxing techniques and movements available in our training library. Each module provides:
  • Step-by-step breakdown of boxing actions and footwork.
  • Visual markers and keypoints for correct posture and form.
  • Real-time feedback on execution and performance.

Boxing Techniques and Training:-

Improve your boxing skills with our detailed analysis and training:

  • Jab: Learn the basics of jabbing, including stance, reach, and timing.
  • Cross: Master the cross punch technique for power and precision.
  • Hook: Understand the mechanics of hook punches and body rotation.
  • Footwork: Practice agile footwork drills for better movement and positioning in the ring.
  • Defense: Study defensive strategies such as blocking, parrying, and slipping.

Our platform aims to enhance your boxing skills through technology-driven analysis and personalized training. Get ready to level up your game!
